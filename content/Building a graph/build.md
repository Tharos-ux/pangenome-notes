## Specification

The reference GFA format specification can be [found here](http://gfa-spec.github.io/GFA-spec/).

## Conversions

You can use `vg convert -gfW` to convert from GFA 1.1 to 1.0 and `vg convert -gf` to convert from 1.0 to 1.1.
